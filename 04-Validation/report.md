. Validation & Controlled Exploitation

. Objective

The objective of this phase was to validate vulnerabilities identified during reconnaissance and vulnerability scanning activities and assess their potential security impact.

Activities Performed

- Tested anonymous FTP authentication
- Enumerated exposed FTP resources
- Validated SMB share accessibility
- Manually verified exposed web resources
- Collected evidence supporting identified findings

Findings

Several services allowed unauthenticated or weakly protected access. Validation activities confirmed the existence of exposed resources and demonstrated potential avenues for unauthorized information access.

Security Impact

The identified weaknesses may allow attackers to access sensitive information, perform reconnaissance, or leverage exposed services for further exploitation.

Skills Demonstrated

- Vulnerability validation
- Evidence collection
- Security impact analysis
- SMB enumeration
- FTP assessment
- Professional security reporting
